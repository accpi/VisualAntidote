About Me

I'm Nelson, a software developer with an emphasis on structured project design and project management. Backing up my technical knowledge and expertise with active listening, flexible critical thinking skills learned from improv, art, and being genuine.

I’m graduating from Humber College’s Computer Programming program this April. At Humber, I’ve learned and worked on a variety of languages, platforms, and tech stacks. You’ll find me at my best working in Java, C#, ASP.NET, and creating strong, well-formed projects that talk to databases through SQL, usually through a RESTful API.
